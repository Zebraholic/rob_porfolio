Do you accept custom orders?

Wholesale Orders: We currently do some wholesale sales to specialty shops and we are open to more wholesale prospects, please contact me for further details.

Custom Orders: Have an idea for an extra special and personal art print? Let's chat! I do custom personalized prints through a collaborative process by taking your idea, input or image reference and create one of a kind stylized art prints. For more information or any questions about the custom process, drop me a note!

Gig Posters: Are you in a band or know someone in a band? we love supporting the live music scene and bands in general and I offer custom limited edition screen printed gig poster design/print/ship services. I offer a very economical cost effective option for bands, please contact me for further details.

What are your shipping terms?

I try to prepare and ship out orders as soon as possible within 24 hours of received payment and make sure to carefully package all my orders to ensure safe and sound arrival to you.

I attempt to provide a good balance between incorporating the most economical shipping rates while ensuring timely receipt of your order. I ship via Canada Post regular parcel and estimated delivery times are as follows:

Shipping within Canada: 3 to 7 days. Shipping to USA via air mail: 1 to 2 weeks. Shipping via air mail to international destinations: 2 to 4 weeks.

I also provide faster shipping options at your request, please inquire about additional shipping cost on a purchase by purchase basis.

What are acceptable payment methods?

My prices are all quoted in US$ dollars.

We accept all forms of online payment at time of purchase through Etsy's online payment system.

Harmonized Sales Tax (HST) is added to Canadian orders based on the rate in your respective province.

Do you accept returns and exchanges?

We make sure to carefully package all my orders to ensure safe and sound arrival to you, but if for some reason, your package gets lost or arrives damaged, I will replace it or refund your purchase. I also include shipping insurance with all my orders so you can rest assured that you as buyer and I as seller are mutually protected against any loss while goods are in transit.

Why are items sometimes out of stock?

Every effort is made to maintain the availability of items shown in the catalogue, but due to popularity and supply issues, some products may not always be available. Generally, IKEA can estimate when a product should be back in stock. Because IKEA products are manufactured throughout the world, there are sometimes circumstances which can cause delivery delays.

When does the new catalogue come out?

The annual catalogue is distributed in August/September of each year and is valid for a full year.
