Left Brain / Right Brain

Kick

Case Study

Following the connection to active street style, the “kick” and “move” story builds on Adidas Active: “move” with style and “kick” with attitude. Through a micro urban inner city focus, cool street style images are presented along with “kick” or “move” taglines and embellished with the ubiquitous three line treatment. Experience roll out starts with OOH treatments including: bus shelter, subway bulletin/junior poster, billboard and also retail display. Consumer connection is then extended with offering a personalized mobile phone cover case either in store or partnered pop up events. Through the Adidas mobile app, consumers are encouraged to upload their own personal photo featuring them wearing Adidas gear and have option of having the “move” and “kick” tagline or have their own name added to the photo. The personalized phone cases are then produced on the spot at event through the use of portable heat transfer sublimation press and presented to consumer. Extension to the experience could also include microsite photo gallery upload.
